Get ready for a high-speed race where the ground is just one option! In BridgeEscape, your goal is to survive as long as possible on a track full of hazards.

## Description

**Bridge Escape** is an endless runner game developed. Players must navigate a dangerous track, collecting coins to unlock new character skins while using gravity manipulation to avoid obstacles and survive as long as possible.<br>

## Features

- **Endless Runner Gameplay**: Run at full speed on a hazardous track
- **Coin Collection System**: Gather coins to unlock new content
- **Unlockable Skins**: Collect coins to unlock different character appearances
- **Gravity Manipulation**: Invert gravity to dodge obstacles
- **High Score System**: Beat your personal record and survive longer
- **Fast-Paced Action**: Test your reflexes in this adrenaline-filled experience

## How to Play

1. Run automatically through the bridge
2. Use controls to jump or invert gravity
3. Collect coins along the way
4. Avoid obstacles and survive as long as possible
5. Beat your high score!
